Our Story

Allie and Noah met in Ocean City on Labor Day weekend through their mutual friend, Brad. As with any trip to Ocean City, there were daytime activities of sun, sand, and waves, followed by nighttime activities of music, food, and Seacrets. After an evening filled with fun, Cam and Dan shared a quiet moment on the beach, under the night stars and the sounds of the crashing waves, culminating in their first kiss.

Upon returning from their beach trip, Allie and Noah continued to chat back and forth, often through email like a reenactment of Tom Hanks and Meg Ryan in “You’ve Got Mail” (personal phones weren’t allowed at Noah’s work). Their first date included a trip to Cheesecake Factory, as Noah loves cheesecake, and ended with a night of bowling where Allie was surprised to see that Noah owned a pair of bowling shoes. Obviously, Allie lost that night.

Allie and Noah love traveling internationally together. Some of their favorite places include the Amalfi Coast, Paris, London, Dubrovnik, and Prague. Even amongst the non-stop sight-seeing and evening cuisine, Cam always made sure there was an opportunity for a daily Gelato. While 2020 was a challenging year for many, Allie and Noah managed to end the year on a high-note, specifically on Christmas Eve. During the evening, while ensuring that all of the presents were under the tree before Christmas day with family, Noah surprised Allie with an early present. With the twinkling of the Christmas lights in the background, Noah popped the question while on one knee and Allie said yes!

We are excited to share the big day, including food, CAKE, drinks, and awkward dancing, with you and our wedding party!
- Cam & Dan
